Breakfast Help


Need help on signing in to Breakfast @ The Highland Cafe(tm)? Use the documentation below:
  1. First, click the Back button below or above

  2. Then, once back at the Help page, click the Home button, at the bottom or the top.

  3. Once back on the home screen, click the Breakfast button.

  4. Scroll down the page and fill in the form to check in for breakfast. Once done, click Submit.*

*Please note that to have breakfast you MUST be on a StayPlan(tm) that includes breakfast.
