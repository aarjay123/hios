Room Key Help


Need help on how to use/get hold of your room key? Please read the documentation below:
  1. First, download the HiOS Mobile app from your app store.*

  2. Sign in with your The Highland Cafe(tm) account by clicking the hamburger menu > Log In [FUNCTIONALITY COMING AT A LATER DATE]

  3. Once you've logged in, your digital room key will appear on the main app page, with the hotel name, location, and your room number[FUNCTIONALITY COMING AT A LATER DATE]

  4. To get into your room, open the HiOS Mobile app, and scan your smartphone against the reader** [FUNCTIONALITY COMING AT A LATER DATE]

*Compatible with smartphones running Android 6 or above, iOS 10 or above
**Scanning function only available on smartphones with NFC
